# Privacy Policy — Khagol (Hindu Panchang & Sky Map)

_Last updated: 2026-06-20_

Khagol is designed to be **completely private and offline**.

## What we collect

**Nothing.** Khagol does not collect, transmit, sell, or share any personal data. It contacts no
server and has no analytics, ads, accounts, or trackers.

## What stays on your device

The following are stored **only in your browser's local storage**, on your device, and are never
sent anywhere:

- Your chosen **location** (city / latitude / longitude / timezone).
- Your chosen **date and time** and display preferences (12h/24h, language mode, ayanamsa, etc.).
- Any **saved dates** you create.

You can clear all of it at any time by removing the extension or clearing its storage in your
browser.

## Permissions

- **`storage`** — to remember your location, date/time and saved dates locally (above).
- **`geolocation`** — only used if you click **📍 Here**. Your coordinates are read in the browser to
  set the observer location for the calculations and are **never transmitted**.

## Data sharing

None. No data ever leaves your browser.

## Contact

For questions about this policy, open an issue on the project's repository.
